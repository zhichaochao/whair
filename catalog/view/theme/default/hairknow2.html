“What is your hair grade?”
Is that the most question you will ask when you start to purchase hair. But is there really hair hair standard in this hair business?
The answer, unfortunately, is no.
Many new buyers will be fool which this answer, because there is many hair suppliers keep tell you their hair grade was the top, and hair quality is the best.
But actually in this hair area. There is no standard hair grade, people will only call the hair grade by what they want. Therefore you will very easy to found that, some hair supper is selling 9A grade, but the price even cheaper than the 6A grade which the other supplier selling. Actually they are the same hair grade.
Due to the muddledness of these hair grade. We are not longer to call the hair by hair grade. But will guide you the correct way to identify the difference between different hair quality.

IN HOTBEAUTYHAIR, WE SELL MAINLY 3 TYPES OF HAIR:

  • Hair name Donor Lasting Can be dyed/bleached Hair Ratio(%)
  • Double Drawn Funmi Hair 1 4-5years yes 100%
  • Raw Brazilian Virgin Hair 1 4-5years yes 80%
  • Virgin Peruvian Hair 2 to 3 3-4years yes 80%
  • Regular Peruvian Hair 3 to 4 2-3years no suggest to bleached 70%
  • Normal Indian Hair 4 to 5 1-2years no suggest to dyed/bleached 60%

In this world, hair material was fixed. The only difference is, is it collected from young girls or elder women. That’s mean the hair can last longer or went bad easily.

Next time when supplier selling you a very high grade hair but with a very questionable cheap price, you should be careful, that might not be the real quality it should be.
